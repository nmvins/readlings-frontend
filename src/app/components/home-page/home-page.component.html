Reading is an important part of our everyday activities. In this world where the technology is continuously evolving, we have to keep up. This is exactly what this app is trying to do. Our brains have different ways of learning, but they can be trained over time. By training our brains to read faster we get a lot of benefits in a lot more areas of our lives.
Speed reading is a technique to increase someone's speed of reading. By using different methods over a long period of time, your brain will be trained to read faster. One of the most important things that will help someone improve their reading skills is getting rid of their inner voice. This app aims to help you do that, increase the speed of reading, increase your level of attention and focus on what you need to know.
