Before you can start training, we have to know a few things about you. Let's start by measuring your level of attention. When you are ready, click on Start. The square will start changing its color. Try to follow the attention square and when you feel like you can't follow it anymore, click on Stop. This, way, we will know how much attention you will be able to pay to what you read today.
Now it's time to start reading. Don't you want to know how much you managed to progress in this training? Sure you do. That's why you have to measure your speed before reading. It's important to know your initial speed. When you are ready, click on Read. After you finish reading, click on Done.
